Our Story

BUILT ON LEGACY.
DRIVEN BY MUSIC.

Seventh Planet Entertainment Group has been connecting incredible talent with the stages, events, and audiences that deserve them for over 16 years. What started as a passion for live music became one of Texas's most trusted booking agencies.

Based in Water Valley, Texas, we've spent over a decade and a half becoming the go-to booking agency across the Lone Star State and beyond. From intimate club shows to massive festival stages, we bring the talent that makes events unforgettable — without breaking the bank.

Led by CEO Keli Pearce, Seventh Planet operates with one simple mission: where talent meets value.
